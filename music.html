a note on music

i listen to music differently depending on what i'm doing. coding and research need something that fades into the background — rhythm without too much lyrical pull. writing needs silence or near-silence. the gym needs actual energy.

frank ocean is the constant across all of it. i've probably listened to blonde more times than any album in my life. it's the kind of thing that sounds different depending on what you're carrying when you put it on.
