My name is Arya Somu.

I'm an applied mathematics and physics undergraduate at UC Berkeley. I'm currently building The Insight Company of California, and I founded Monolith Systematic, a systematic global macro fund. I'm also affiliated with Stanford's Advanced Financial Technologies Lab.

I'm currently writing The Hardware Almanac, an essay series on the transition from software-defined power to hardware-defined power, and why every institution meant to govern the modern world is not equipped to survive it.

In the past, I co-founded Aletheia Laboratories as Head of Applied Science, was a quantitative researcher at Esplanade Capital, and an astrophysicist at Arizona State's BEUS Center.
